Tasting Room


Rated the best new restaurant of 2018 in the Calgary Herald, The Dandy Brewing Company and Tasting Room is a unique dining experience located in the heart of Ramsay.


The modern space features 20 taps of Dandy Beers (maybe a few guest taps), a curated cocktail list featuring local spirit, and a daily selection of top quality wines available by the glass.



Tours are available for private groups. Please email info@dandybrewing.com for more information and booking.


Dandy does it's best to keep stock of all product packaged on site for offsales. Please see the fridge beside the bar for up to date selection.


The tasting room does fill growlers on site of select beers. Please contact us beforehand through phone for up to date offerings.


Note: We do not currently carry empty growlers, so please bring your own, clean, growler for fills.



Open Daily for lunch and dinner service.



We are pleased and able to accommodate most dietary needs, Please notify your server.
