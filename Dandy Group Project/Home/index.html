Calgary's original little brewery

We produce inspired ales, with lots of care.
Our unique approach to brewing and keen eye to detail fosters creativity in both our recipes and beer styles.
On site, we are pleased to offer a unique brewery experience featuring a full service tasting room restaurant with an exquisite menu of fine dining drinking food, a curated selection of cocktails, glass pours of selected wines, and 20 taps of Dandy beers.
We share a passion for not only brewing, but the people, process, and experiences that make the experience so special.
