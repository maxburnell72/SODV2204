Dennis Rodman

Basketball Career

Dennis Rodman is an American Basketball player who played in the NBA he played for numerous teams over the years some of which included the San Antonia Spurs, The Detroit Pistons, the Chicago bulls, And the Los Angela’s Lakers. He Helped the Bulls and the Pistons win multiple NBA championships and was known as one of the leagues best rebounders. Rodman was Drafted in 1986 by the Detroit pistons. The Pistons won 2 Championships with Rodman in 1987 and 1990. A few years after this he was traded to the san Antonio spurs and later traded to the Chicago bulls where he played along side Scotty pippin and Michael Jordan Where they Won 3 Consecutive Championships in 96, 97 and 98. Dennis Retired in 2000 with the Detroit Mavericks and was inducted into the NBA hall of fame in 2011.

Early Life

Dennis was Born in Trenton New Jersey in 1961 he had a rough up bringing and lived through poverty most of his life. In high school Dennis was Only 5'6 And was turned away from the high school football and basketball team. After high school he got a job as a janitor and grew to be 6'7 He then caught the attention of a college recruit who spotted him on the local basketball courts and offered him a position to play for the school which is what really kickstarted his basketball career.

The unlikely Freindship of Kim Jong Un and Dennis Rodman

One of the things that made Dennis rodman relevant again in modern time is his unusual friendship he developed with North Korean communist dictator Kim Jong Un. Kim Has been known to be a huge American basketball fan despite his distaste for the country itself. Kim’s Favorite team was always the Chicago Bulls. The greatest Chicago bulls team, and the one Kim watched growing up who won the three championships was the one Dennis rodman played on. Dennis also happened to be Kim’s Favorite player. After the NBA Dennis played for the Harlem globetrotters 3 of which took a trip with vice news to visit the communist dictator. The 2 got along very well and Dennis told Kim that he will always have a friend for life and from there the friendship blossomed. Since then Dennis has flown out numerous times to visit his good friend Kim and apparently hangout all the time. They have gone skiing together riden horses together, Sang karaoke, watched basketball and played basketball. The 2 have remained friends for years and Dennis has also helped bridge the gap between Kim Jong un and US Leader Donald trump.
